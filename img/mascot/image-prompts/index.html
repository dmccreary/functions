    Base Character Description

    Rick is a friendly cartoon raccoon mascot for an IB Mathematics Functions textbook. He is a compact, round raccoon with classic gray, brown, tan and black fur, a bushy striped tail, and his signature black mask markings around bright, clever eyes. Rick has no hat, no clothing, and nothing in his hands — just a simple, expressive raccoon with a mischievous grin.
