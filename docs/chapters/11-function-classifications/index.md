# Function Classifications

## Summary

This chapter classifies functions by their structural properties, an AHL focus area. Students learn to verify even and odd function symmetry algebraically, explore periodic functions, and study the modulus (absolute value) function and its graphical modifications including $y = |f(x)|$, $y = f(|x|)$, and $y = [f(x)]^2$. The chapter also covers piecewise-defined functions including step functions, the signum function, and the greatest integer function. Function behavior analysis ties together concepts of increasing/decreasing behavior and concavity.
